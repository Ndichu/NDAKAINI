How to get Started?

This how we do it. Follow the steps provided like a pro

Step One

Register for the marathon and Collect your bibs in the registration area at given stations on 6, 7, or 8 September.

Step Two

Get your participant a T-shirt at the Ndakaini Gounds. Storage location for your sports bag on race daywill be provided

Step Three

Prepare your attire: Changing rooms are available and will be provided for everyone.

Step Four

Water, first aid, and toilet stations are available along with the different courses.

Step Five

Pacer teams are available for you to achieve your personal best time in the marathon distance.

Finally

Results will be posted on the website after you finish and awards will be goven respectivley

About Us

The Ndakaini Half Marathon event, which includes a 21km race, 10km race, and a 5km family fun run, is held every year in September/October around Ndakaini Dam, the source of over 70% of Nairobi's water supply.
